## Problem

Trading review was split across too many surfaces: broker CSVs, intraday journal entries, Whoop readiness, and weekly notes in a separate knowledge base. Numbers lived in one place; psychology and discipline lived in another — so patterns were visible in hindsight but hard to act on during the week.

## Goal

Build a single **operator dashboard** that connects trades, session behaviour, readiness, and structured weekly synthesis — framed as a process tool, not a signal service or performance pitch.
